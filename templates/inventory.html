Connect Your WooCommerce Store

This tool analyzes your real sales data to tell you what to buy, what to discount, and where your cash is tied up. Connect your WooCommerce store to get started.

Setup in 3 steps:

1. In WooCommerce → Settings → Advanced → REST API, create a new key with Read permissions.

2. Set these environment variables:

export WOOCOMMERCE_URL="https://your-shop.com" export WOOCOMMERCE_KEY="ck_your_consumer_key" export WOOCOMMERCE_SECRET="cs_your_consumer_secret"

3. Restart the app and click Refresh Data.

Read-only access. We never modify your store data. All analysis runs locally.
